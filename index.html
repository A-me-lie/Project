Les Sous-doués en vacances is a 1982 French comedic film, directed by Claude Zidi. It is the sequel to Les sous-doués (1980).The film was shot between 10 August and 3 October 1981.[1] Roland Moreno, the real life inventor of the smart card, was cast in the film as a "mad inventor" who creates a "love computer."[2]

La Boum 2 is a 1982 French teen romantic comedy film directed by Claude Pinoteau and starring Claude Brasseur, Brigitte Fossey, and Sophie Marceau. Written by Danièle Thompson and Claude Pinoteau, the film is about a girl who falls in love with a boy and must deal with the question of making love for the first time. La Boum 2 is the sequel to 1980's La Boum (The Party)
